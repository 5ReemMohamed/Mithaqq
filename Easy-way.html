About Easy Way

A subsidiary of Mithaq Business Development Holding Company, we offer all domestic and international ticket booking services through Easy Way Travel and Tourism, at the best prices and with the highest quality facilitation services. We also offer hotel booking services to ensure a comfortable trip and a pleasant stay, as well as the best tourism, Hajj, and Umrah offers.

Our vision is to expand the use of all flight and hotel booking methods while providing high-end travel services at affordable prices.
