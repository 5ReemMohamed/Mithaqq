About Mithaqq Global

Mithaqq Business Development Holding Company was established in 2016 with a promising vision and strategic ideas to ensure individual and institutional success, based on specific standards, systems, and clear objectives for creating and building entrepreneurs in various fields and specialties, achieving intellectual, financial, and social success and achieving great leadership in the world of finance and business.
